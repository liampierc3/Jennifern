ðŸ’§ General Watering Tips for All Plants:

  • Drainage is Key: Ensure all pots have drainage holes. If the pots don't have holes, your mom will need to repot them into pots that do, or be extremely careful not to let water sit in the bottom.
  • Don't Guess: Always check the soil moisture with your finger before watering.
  • Adjust to Seasons: Plants generally need less water in the winter when light levels are lower and growth slows down. They'll need more water in spring and summer during active growth.
  • Light Matters: Plants in brighter light will generally need more frequent watering than those in lower light.
