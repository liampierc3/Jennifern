üíß How to water it perfectly

The Golden Rule:

Identical care to other snake plants - this is a drought-tolerant succulent that stores water in its thick, beautiful leaves.

Frequency:

Allow soil to dry out completely between waterings. Every 2-4 weeks is typical, but less in winter. The variegated leaves don't change the watering needs!

Special Note:

Overwatering can cause the beautiful variegation to fade or the plant to develop root rot. Keep it on the dry side to maintain those stunning colors!

üé® Keeping Those Colors Vibrant

Light for Color:

Bright, indirect light helps maintain the golden variegation. Too little light may cause colors to fade over time.

Avoid Direct Sun:

Direct sunlight can scorch the beautiful variegated leaves and cause brown spots. Bright, filtered light is perfect.

Patience is Key:

Variegated plants grow slower than their solid-colored cousins. This is normal and helps maintain the striking patterns!
