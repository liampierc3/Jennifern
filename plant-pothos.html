üíß How to water it perfectly

The Golden Rule:

Pothos prefers its soil to dry out partially between waterings - not bone dry, but not soggy either.

Frequency:

Water when the top 1-2 inches of soil feel dry to the touch. This could be every 1-2 weeks depending on your home's conditions.

Plant Communication:

Pothos will tell you it's thirsty! The leaves start to look slightly droopy or dull when it needs water. Water it, and it should perk right back up within hours!
