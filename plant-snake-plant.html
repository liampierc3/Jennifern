üíß How to water it perfectly

The Golden Rule:

Like other snake plants, this is a drought-tolerant succulent. Overwatering is its biggest enemy - it stores water in its thick leaves.

Frequency:

Allow the soil to dry out completely between waterings. This could be every 2-4 weeks, or even less frequently in winter when growth slows down.

The "Neglect Rule":

When in doubt, it's better to underwater this plant than to overwater. It can go for a surprisingly long time without water and will thank you for it!
